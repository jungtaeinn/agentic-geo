# E-E-A-T Guidance v1

Use E-E-A-T as a trust filter, not as decoration.

- Experience: distinguish firsthand review patterns from product claims.
- Expertise: prefer papers, official documents, and structured product data for technical or efficacy claims.
- Authoritativeness: do not invent authority. If authority is missing, state the limitation.
- Trust: keep evidence references and unsupported claims in diagnostics.

For beauty, wellness, or health-adjacent products, avoid definitive treatment language unless the evidence explicitly supports it and the requested market allows it.
