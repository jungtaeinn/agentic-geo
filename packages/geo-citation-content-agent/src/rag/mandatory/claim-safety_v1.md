# Claim Safety v1

Strong claims need evidence. If evidence is weak or unavailable, downgrade the wording.

Avoid:

- cure, treat, guaranteed, proven, best, miracle
- 완치, 치료, 보장, 입증된, 최고, 기적
- definitive medical, legal, financial, or efficacy conclusions
- hidden advertising language
- pressure CTAs such as buy now, shop here, limited offer, link in bio

Prefer:

- may help
- appears most relevant for
- reviews often mention
- the available evidence supports
- I would be careful about
- worth comparing against
